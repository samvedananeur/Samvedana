About Dr. Mauli Vora

Dr. Mauli Vora is a highly skilled neurologist with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating complex neurological disorders. With her compassionate approach and evidence-based practice, she provides personalized care to each patient.

After completing her medical education and specialization, Dr. Vora has worked with some of the most prestigious medical institutions in India, gaining expertise in various subspecialties of neurology.

  • MBBS - Top Medical College
  • MD (Medicine) - Prestigious University
  • DNB (Neurology) - National Board of Examinations
  • Fellowship in Epilepsy Management
  • Special Training in Stroke Care
